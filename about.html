About

I am the Frederick H. Rawson Distinguished Service Professor of Medicine and Computer Science and the Jim and Karen Frank Director of the Center for Translational Data Science (CTDS) at the University of Chicago.

I have been a Partner of Analytic Strategy Partners LLC since 2017. From 2002 - 2016, I was the founder and Managing Partner of Open Data Group. Open Data Group provides services and products so that companies can build and deploy machine learning and AI models.
